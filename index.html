🧬 Research Bio

Data-driven solutions for hydrological challenges.

With a strong foundation in geospatial science data analytics, I specialise in water conservation and sustainable resource management through advanced applications of Remote Sensing, GIS, and Machine Learning.

Raised in a water-scarce region, I am driven to develop scalable solutions that address hydrological challenges and support resilience in drought-prone areas. My work transforms complex datasets into actionable insights for watershed management, groundwater monitoring, and water resource optimisation.
