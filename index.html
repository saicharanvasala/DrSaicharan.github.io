Research Bio

With a strong foundation in geospatial science data analytics, I specialise in water conservation and sustainable resource management through advanced applications of Remote Sensing, GIS, and Machine Learning.

Raised in a water-scarce region, I am driven to develop scalable, data-driven solutions that address hydrological challenges and support resilience in drought-prone and climate-sensitive areas. My work focuses on transforming complex hydrological and remote sensing datasets into actionable insights for improved watershed management, groundwater monitoring, and water resource optimisation, while also mentoring future professionals in the geospatial sector.
